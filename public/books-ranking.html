My Favourite Books

My top books reccomendations:

1. Eat Pray Love

by Elizabeth Gilbert

A 30 years old women leaves her husband and go on a full year trip in Italy, India and Indonesia. A great book for singles.

2. When Nietzsche Wept

by Irvin Yalom

A great novel occuring in Venice in 1882 about the first buds of psychotherapy.

3. Men Are from Mars, Women Are from Venus

by John Gray

The book discusses the fundamental psychological differences between men and women and related common relationship problems and ways to deal with them.
